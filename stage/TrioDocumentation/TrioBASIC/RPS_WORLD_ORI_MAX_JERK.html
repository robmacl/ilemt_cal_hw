WORLD_ORI_MAX_JERK

Type:

Robotics Parameter

Description:

The WORLD_ORI_MAX_JERK axis parameter can be used to read and write the maximum possible orientation axis jerk in world mode.

JERK is the derivative of acceleration. JERK units are /sec3.  The way JERK is applied to the axis motion depends on the value of VP_MODE .

Value:

The robot axis maximum orientation acceleration in degrees / sec 3.
