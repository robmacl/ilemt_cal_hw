SYNC_PAUSE

Type:

Axis Parameter

Syntax:

SYNC_PAUSE AXIS(n) = 60 {time period in servo period units}

Description:

SYNC_PAUSE is one of 2 new axis parameters that allow greater control over when the sync motion occurs during the sync time period.

Value:

The time period for the pause in milliseconds
