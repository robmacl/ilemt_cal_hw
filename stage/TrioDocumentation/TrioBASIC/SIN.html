SIN

Type:

Mathematical Function

Syntax:

value = SIN(expression)

Description:

Returns the natural sine of the supplied angle measured in radians.

Parameters:

value:

The natural sine of the expression

expression:

Any valid TrioBASIC numeric expression representing an angle measured in radians

Example:

Print the natural sine of 1 radian on the command line

>>PRINT SIN(1)

0.8415

>>
