EtherCAT Drive Monitor

The Monitor page allows read-only access to drive properties which are periodically updated to give a live view on the drive.

Items

The items shown are gouped into logical families, these are:

  • Drive, the drive environment
  • Control, inputs and outputs of the various control laws
  • Protection, status of drive protection algorithms
  • Digital I/O, state of the digital inputs (including touch probe) and outputs
  • Encoder, position and velocity of the encoder channels

The values tabulated showing the value name, the value and the units for each line item enabled.

Page control

There are various page control icons shown at the top pf the page which control what items are visible and the update rate.
