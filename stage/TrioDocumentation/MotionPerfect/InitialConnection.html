Initial Connection

To make the initial connection to a controller:

  1. Make sure that your controller is powered up and connected to the computer
  2. Start Motion Perfect 3. Once it has started up the initial screen should be displayed.
  3. Select "Connect in Direct mode" from the "Controller" menu. As Motion Perfect has not been connected before the "Connection" dialog will be displayed.
  4. Select the communications interface used by your controller (this will usualy be Ethernet), then enter it's parameters. For an Ethernet connection this will be the IP address (defailt 192.168.0.250) and the TCP port (default 23).
  5. Click on the "Apply & Connect" button. The "Connect" will close and Motion Perfect will go into Direct Mode with an active Terminal tool.

Motion Perfect will remember the last used connection parameters and will automatically try and use them when reconnecting in Direct Mode in the future.
