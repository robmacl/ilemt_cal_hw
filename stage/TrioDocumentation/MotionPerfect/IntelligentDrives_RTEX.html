Intelligent Drives – RTEX

RTEX

RTEX is an Ethernet based communications system for industrial control developed by the Panasonic Corporation of Japan. It is largely used to communicate with drives produced by Panasonic.

Drives Supported

Panasonic A4N, A5N and A6N series RTEX drives.
